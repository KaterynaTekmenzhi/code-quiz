Welcome to Code Quiz!

This is a quiz game that tests your knowledge of HTML and CSS. You will be given a series of questions to answer. You will have a limited amount of time to answer each question. If you answer a question incorrectly, you will lose a point. If you answer a question correctly, you will gain a point. The game ends when you run out of time or when you answer all of the questions correctly. You will be able to see your score at the end of the game.

Good luck!
